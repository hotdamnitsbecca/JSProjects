About Me

Rebecca is an enthusiastic creator of everything from code to cuisine. Her philosophy is if you can DIY you should DIY. She appreciates mid-century design, craft beer and board games.

Rebecca loves cooking, and has recently begun eating meat after 10 years as a vegetarian, so she is discovering a new world of delicious food. An obsession with puzzles has lead her to indulge in activities from D&D (she was briefly a dungeon master), a Sherlock Holmes obsession, and DASH. She also loves coding, using Python mostly, but is fluent in Java, C++, and Matlab as well. Right now she is pretty obsessed with Lilypad projects.

Rebecca works in Systems Integration at a biotech company that makes genome sequencing instruments. She enjoys working on the complex problems at the intersection of hardware, software and chemistry that occur in instrument development. She also loves developing software tools for her and her coworkers and teasing out meaning from large datasets.

Rebecca is semi-social
