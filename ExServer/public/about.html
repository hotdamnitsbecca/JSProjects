About Me

I am a graduate from the University of Pittsburgh where I majored in chemical engineering. I received minors in polymer engineering, chemistry, and physics and a certificate in nuclear engineering. During my studies, I contributed to the Li Lab doing surface energy testing of CVD graphene. I worked as a teaching assistant and a student library aid for the Bevier Engineering Library. I am now employed at Illumina in Systems Integration.

I hail from the Lone Star state where all things are bigger and better. You can now find me in beautiful San Diego! I love spending hours on crafting and building projects, learning about new concepts, and being outside.
